Before You Start

You'll need to be logged in to each platform on your phone or computer as you go through these steps. Have your logins ready for:

1

Connect Facebook & Instagram via Meta Business Suite

business.facebook.com — manages both platforms in one place

Free
a. Go to business.facebook.com and log in with your personal Facebook account
b. Click Settings → Business Assets → Add Assets and add your Facebook Page and Instagram account
c. Go to Settings → Partners → Add Partner and enter this Partner ID: 309317162040251
d. Under Partner permissions, enable: Manage Page, Create Ads, View Insights — then click Save

Why this way?

Adding us as a Partner means we manage your accounts through our own Business Portfolio — you never share a password and you can remove access at any time in one click.

2

Switch Instagram to a Business Account

Required for scheduling, analytics, and contact buttons to work

Instagram
a. Open the Instagram app → tap your profile picture → tap the three lines ☰ in the top right corner
b. Go to Settings & Privacy → Account type → Switch to Professional Account
c. Choose Business (not Creator) → select your category → connect your Facebook Business Page when prompted
d. Once done, Instagram will be accessible through the Meta Business Suite connection in Step 1 — no extra steps needed
3

Add a Manager to Google Business Profile

business.google.com — for posts, photos, and review responses

Google
a. Go to business.google.com and sign in with the Google account that owns your business listing
b. Click Business Profile Settings → Managers → Add
c. Enter trimmarketing01@gmail.com and set the role to Manager
d. Click Invite — we'll accept from our end and will have access within minutes
4

Add a LinkedIn Page Admin

For B2B content, fleet case studies, and company page management

LinkedIn
a. Go to your LinkedIn Company Page and click Admin Tools → Manage Admins in the top right
b. Click Add Admin → search for Don Boone or enter donboone3@gmail.com
c. Set role to Super Admin and click Save
5

Share Yelp for Business Access

biz.yelp.com — Yelp doesn't support multi-user admin, use a password manager

Yelp
a. Go to biz.yelp.com and confirm your business listing is claimed and up to date
b. Share your login credentials securely using 1Password or LastPass — never send passwords via text or email
c. If you don't have a password manager, we recommend setting up a free 1Password account — it takes 2 minutes and keeps your credentials safe
6

Approve Buffer Connection

buffer.com — we'll send you a team invite once steps 1–5 are complete

Scheduling
a. We'll send a Buffer team invite to your email once the platform connections above are set up
b. Accept the invite — this gives you visibility into everything we schedule so you always know what's going out
c. You can approve, edit, or pause any post before it goes live — full transparency, always

Common Questions

Do you need my passwords?

No — for Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn we use official admin/partner access. You only share a password for Yelp, and we recommend doing that through a password manager like 1Password.

Can I remove access later?

Yes, at any time. Each platform lets you remove a manager or partner in under 60 seconds. You're always in control.

Will I be able to see what gets posted?

Yes — through Buffer you'll have full visibility into the content calendar. You can approve, edit, or remove any post before it goes live.

I'm stuck on a step — what do I do?

Text or email us and we'll jump on a quick screen share to walk you through it. Most issues are resolved in under 10 minutes.
